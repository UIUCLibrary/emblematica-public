For all other uses, individual items may be subject to additional conditions of use, according to the policies of the institutions holding the physical items and/or their digital surrogates. Additional details regarding conditions of use for specific sub-collections of emblem books included in the Emblematica Online Digital Virtual Collection can be found on the holding institution’s website (listed below).

  • University of Glasgow
  • University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign Rare Book & Manuscript Library

When using resources from this site, please acknowledge as, “Courtesy of the Emblematica Online Digital Collection and the _____ Library of the _____,” filling in the blanks with the name of the library and institution contributing the resource. (For example, “Courtesy of the Emblematica Online Digital Collection and The Rare Book & Manuscript Library of the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign.”)
